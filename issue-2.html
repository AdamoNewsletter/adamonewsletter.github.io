ADAMO Newsletter.

Games Development
A brand new Gaming Development programme and department has been added to ADAMO'S ever growing family, consisting of a small (set to grow) team of motivated game lovers. Taylor Jarvis, alongside Ben Payne and newcomers Ash Higgins and Kate Coleman have begun learning new software that in the future will mould the GD department. After previously teasing that the team's first ever game is under construction, we are now allowed to share that the development of the Tower Defence game is coming along smoothly, and that the progress of the characters featured in the game is all original artwork. By the end of the month a playable game should be up and running, and to add to the excitement all ADAMO employees will have the early access to test the game!

GP and MCB’s content creator merge has happened and hopefully the new members of MCB are settling in well and can continue to produce quality content whilst collaborating with many new editors and content creators. Games Performance is Building Brands!
Very productive past couple of weeks for GP. The focus has been on setting up everyone’s individual streams and helping them to establish their own unique branding. A warm welcome to two new member of the team;  Lilith Burgham https://www.twitch.tv/fleurfps who masterfully plays Apex Legends and Pearce Blair https://www.twitch.tv/blairgames/videos who is an expert in Call of Duty. Remember to check their, and all other GP member’s discord profiles for notifications on when they are live and be sure to go follow and give some support as every bit helps!

New merch is on the way
Suppliers have been researched for both Adamo & TENSTAR merchandise to be able to bulk buy products in the future, this will help action on the feedback about shipping costs. Furthermore, more products have been worked on as per the feedback from the sheet that was sent out across the company, including a look into producing beanies & caps as these came up multiple times in the sheet’s results. Also, large mouse mats are on the way! They are in the final stage of quality testing before a final decision can be made but expect to see them in the store soon.

TENSTAR merch has also been officially designed and is ready to go, once Gamers Apparel have it up on their end then expect to see the TENSTAR merch launched, hopefully by the end of the month.

SMT Player Cards
Player cards are a brand new concept coming soon, these cards are similar to a CV but they have a more visually pleasing aesthetic to look at. Templates are already being created by Shaun Foster whilst Rosie Bell and Josh Clarke gather the relevant information to include on them. These cards can be used as an alternative for a CV if employees wish or they can be kept as memorabilia for the duration you have worked at ADAMO.
